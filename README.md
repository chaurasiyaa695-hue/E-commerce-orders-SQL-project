# E-commerce Orders SQL Project

SQL-based analysis of an e-commerce orders dataset, covering customer spending, product performance, delivery times, payments, and monthly trends.

## Dataset columns
- **Order info** – Order_ID, Customer_ID, Customer_Name, City, Product
- **Product info** – Category, Price, Quantity, Total_Amount, Payment_Method
- **Dates & Status** – Order_Date, Delivery_Date, Rating, Order_Status, Delivery_Days

## Queries included
- Top customers by total spend
- Top 5 customers by spend
- Customers with more than 3 orders
- Most ordered product / least ordered product
- Orders delivered in more than 5 days
- Payment method usage breakdown
- Average rating per product
- Orders per month / revenue per month
- Highest spending city
- Orders above the average order amount

## Key Insights
- **Top city** – Bangalore leads with ₹45.3L revenue, highest among all cities
- **Top customer** – Customer_1005 is the highest spender at ₹6,33,451
- **Most ordered product** – Phone (48 orders); least ordered – Headphones (33 orders)
- **Payment preference** – Cash is most used (29%); Credit Card is least used (19.5%)
- **Best/worst month** – December had the most orders (21); September the fewest (12)
- **Ratings** – Tablet rated highest (3.16 avg); Laptop needs improvement (2.85 avg)

## Tools used
SQL (queries run and validated with result grids), presentation compiled in PowerPoint/WPS
